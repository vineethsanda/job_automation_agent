## ✨ New Capabilities

### 1. **Automatic Email Replies** 📧
When recruiters email you AFTER the agent starts:
- ✅ Email detected automatically  
- ✅ Reply generated by Ollama LLM
- ✅ Email sent to recruiter instantly
- ✅ All within 5-10 seconds!

### 2. **Automatic Job Inquiry Emails** 💼
When new jobs post on LinkedIn AFTER the agent starts:
- ✅ Job discovered automatically
- ✅ Professional inquiry email generated by LLM
- ✅ Email sent to recruiter/careers contact
- ✅ You become the first to apply!

### 3. **Smart Timestamp Filtering** ⏰
- ✅ Only handles items arriving AFTER agent startup
- ✅ Existing emails/jobs still require manual approval (safety feature)
- ✅ Prevents accidental replies to ancient emails
